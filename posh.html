PoSH - Compliance

At Cactus Social Education Health and Research Foundation, we prioritize compliance with PoSH (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) regulations. We are committed to fostering a safe and respectful work environment where every individual feels valued and protected. Our PoSH compliance policies are meticulously designed to prevent and address instances of sexual harassment, ensuring the well-being and dignity of all members of our community. Through continuous training and awareness programs, we empower our employees to recognize, report, and effectively respond to any potential violations of PoSH guidelines. Upholding PoSH compliance is not just a legal obligation for us; it's an integral part of our commitment to promoting a culture of equality, respect, and inclusivity.
