Training

MSW Students Internship Program

  • Our hybrid internship model allows students to engage in both on-site and remote internship experiences, catering to individual preferences and circumstances.
  • In-person internships provide students with the opportunity to work directly with communities, organizations, and stakeholders, gaining firsthand experience in various social work settings.
  • Virtual internships offer flexibility and accessibility, allowing students to participate in projects, research, and advocacy efforts from anywhere with an internet connection.

Project Proposal Writing

  • Effective project proposal writing is essential for securing funding and support for social initiatives. In this training component, students will learn the fundamentals of proposal development, including needs assessment, goal setting, budgeting, and stakeholder engagement.
  • Through interactive workshops and practical exercises, students will develop the skills and confidence to draft compelling project proposals that align with the objectives and priorities of funding agencies and organizations.

Monitoring and Evaluation

  • Monitoring and evaluation are critical aspects of project management, ensuring accountability, learning, and continuous improvement. In this training component, students will learn how to design and implement monitoring and evaluation frameworks to assess the impact and effectiveness of social programs.
  • Topics covered include developing indicators, data collection methods, data analysis, reporting, and utilization of findings for decision-making and program improvement.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

  • Corporate social responsibility (CSR) plays a significant role in promoting sustainable development and social change. This training component explores the principles and practices of CSR, highlighting the role of businesses and organizations in addressing social and environmental challenges.
  • Students will examine case studies, engage in discussions, and learn how to develop CSR initiatives that create shared value for businesses, communities, and society at large.
